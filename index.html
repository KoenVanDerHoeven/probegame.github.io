About ProbeGame

Race to be the winner of the cold war space program. Choose to play from USSR, USA, Japan, EU, India and Ethiopia as you allocate resources and work your way to be the first in space. Whether you take the noble scientific approach, or go for a more rogue espionage attempt, be the one to rewrite history as you descend up into the stars.
